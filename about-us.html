About Us

History

In 2000 Matt Powell was an ambitious 21 year old putting in 18-hour days as a journeyman plumber. He had $1,000 in his pocket and a vision to bring superior plumbing service and craftsmanship to the Greater Richmond area. Fast-forward 15 years, and he's owner of a successful company with over 10 crews and 25 employees and an annual revenue of three million dollars.

In August of 2000, after receiving his master plumber's license, Matt founded Imperial Plumbing, Inc. Operating out of a storage shed with a handful of employees, at first Matt focused on new construction. But it wasn't long before he branched out into full-service repair and restoration for homes and businesses.

Our Mission

We love what we do and it shows in our work. Every day we strive to provide the caliber of service and products that make our customers more than satisfied clients, but advocates. We provide complete clarity and transparency on all jobs before we begin so there's never any surprises. We go the extra mile for our customers because we know it is our customers who help fuel our success.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to positively impact each client through 100% satisfacation, allowing them to honestly support and help us turn Imperial Plumbing, Inc. into a household brand throughout Greater Richmond area and beyond! We love what we do and it shows through our work.
